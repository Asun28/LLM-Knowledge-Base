# Obsidian

Local-first markdown knowledge tool used by Karpathy as the viewing layer over his LLM-maintained wiki.

## Key Facts

- Karpathy uses Obsidian to browse the LLM-compiled wiki (source: [[raw/articles/karpathy-x-post.md]]).
- Images referenced by raw sources are downloaded locally so the LLM can resolve them inside Obsidian.
- Marp slide shows and matplotlib images rendered from queries are also viewed in Obsidian.
